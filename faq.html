Frequently asked

Product

Where is Hermosa jewellery made?

All our jewelry is handcrafted in our atelier in Ivano-Frankivsk (Ukraine).

Product

Does your jewelry wear off after showering or swimming?

Never! We only use solid gold (not vermeil or plated gold) in our jewlery to ensure it stays intact for everyday wear. You don't have to take them off while showering, swimming, sleeping or working out . You can wear our jewelry 24/7 without the jewelry losing its shine or tarnishing in any way.

Product

Are your gold conflict-free?

Yes! Our traceable gold, also known as ‘green gold’, comes from conflict-free regions, adhering to strict environmental and human rights policies.

Product

Are you using vermeil or plated gold in your jewllery?

Never! Our jewelry is made out of solid gold, ONLY !

Product

Does your gold contains any nickel of zinc?

Our gold is completely zinc-free. Our yellow and rose gold is always nickel-free, while our white gold contains a tiny amount of it.

Product

Why are you using solid 14k gold?

We use 14k gold because it scratches less and doesn’t bent or wear out as easily as 18K gold. Solid 14k gold comes at a much fairer price without diminishing its aesthetic.

Sizing

I'm not sure what stud size to get?

We recommend going to your favourite Hermosa earring in our shop. When selecting a stud size, all the pictures will automatically change to showcase the size you selected.

Sizing

What's the length of your golden chain?

Our Hermosa solid gold chain can be worn in 3 lengths; choker look, mid-stack or a looser fit - it's adjustable at 15” (37cm), 17”(42cm) and 18” (45cm). Perfect for stacking.

Sizing

Can I wear the add-ons with my own studs or chain?

For earrings: all of our add-ons are made to fit all of our Hermosa studs perfectly. We can't guarantee your own studs will fit our add-ons.
For necklaces: all of our add-ons are made to go through our Hermosa chain perfectly. We can't guarantee that the add-ons will go through your own chain.

Returns

Can I return or exchange my piece and will I be refunded?

Of course! You are allowed to exchange or return your pieces 30 days from delivery. Just make sure that the items are returned unworn and in the original packaging.

Returns

Are returns or exchanges free?

Yes! Returns or exchanges are always on us! You won't have to pay any shipping fee and will be fully refunded as soon as we receive your package.

Returns

How do I initiate a return or exchange?

Send us an e-mail with your order number to hello@hermosa.com and we will provide you with a return label and the shipping instructions.

Returns

The full amount of your return will be refunded within a maximum of 5 days after receiving the piece in our atelier for verification.
